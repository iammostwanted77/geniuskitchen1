Ingredients of Gluten Free Banana & Blueberry Muffins

  1. Prepare 2 cup of Gluten Free Flour.
  2. You need 2 tsp of Baking Powder.
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of Softened Butter.
  5. It's 1 cup of Sugar.
  6. It's 2 large of Eggs.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of Organic Milk.
  9. It's 1/4 tsp of nutmeg, grated.
  10. Prepare 2 cup of Blueberries.
  11. You need 1 large of Banana.

Gluten Free Banana & Blueberry Muffins instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Mix wet ingredients together.
  3. Leave the Blueberries until the very end.
  4. With a mixer add the wet and dry together and then the banana.
  5. Once the mixture is smooth fold in the BlueBerries.
  6. Put into 18 individual muffin tins or cups.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes.
  8. Let cool then serve.
