Ingredients of Lemon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins

  1. Prepare of Ingredients.
  2. It's 1 3/4 cup of flour.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of sugar.
  4. You need 2 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  5. It's 1 of egg, beaten.
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup of milk.
  7. You need 1/3 cup of cooking oil.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of chopped pecans.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of blueberries.
  10. It's 1 cup of container lemon yogurt.

Lemon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins instructions

  1. mix together dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. make well in center.
  3. mix together milk, egg, oil, yogurt.
  4. add to dry ingredients.
  5. stir until well blended.
  6. mixture should be lumpy.
  7. add pecans and blueberries.
  8. carefully fold into mixture.
  9. fill greased muffin pans..
  10. bake at 400Â° F for 15- 20 minutes or until golden.
  11. NOTE : if using mini muffin pan baking time may be shorter. check after 10 minutes.
  12. serve warm and enjoy.
