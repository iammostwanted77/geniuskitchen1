Ingredients of Ultimate twice baked potatoes

  1. Prepare 2 of Potatoes.
  2. Prepare 1 oz of Olive oil.

  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of Milk.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of Butter.
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of Cream.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of Cheddar cheese.
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup of Bacon.
  9. You need 1/4 cup of Green onions.

Ultimate twice baked potatoes instructions

  1. Preheat oven 400°.
  2. Rub down potatoes with olive oil and poke them with a fork.
  3. On the top or mid rack, place potatoes. Cook 1h 15m..
  4. Remove potatoes and let cool for 10-15 minutes..
  5. Cut off 1/3 off the top. And remove the insides. Leave about 1/4 inch along the sides..
  6. Combine potato, sour cream, milk, cream, and butter. Mash or use a hand mixer to combine (if using the mixer do not over mix!).
  7. With a spoon, mix in bacon, cheese and green onions. Fill back up skins..
  8. Reduce the oven temp to 350°. Cook 15-20 minutes..
  9. Remove and let cool 5 minutes. Serve!.
