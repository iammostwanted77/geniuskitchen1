Tito's seafood gumbo instructions

  1. Create a roux by gradually heating oil while adding all purpase flour continually stirring. When the roux becomes dark like the appearance of chocolate, the rue is done..
  2. Add okra to the roux and saute until lightly browned. Add the bell peppers, cellery, onion and saute lightly. Add the chicken, ham, hot sausage, hot sausage links and saute for 5 to 10 minutes..
  3. Incorporate chicken broth, claim juice, bay leaves and let simmer. Add salt and tyme to your taste..
  4. After the brew reaches a boil add the crabs, shrimps, oysters and reduce to a simmer. Serve over long grain rice and enjoy..
