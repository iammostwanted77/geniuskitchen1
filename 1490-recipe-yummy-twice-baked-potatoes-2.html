Ingredients of Twice Baked Potatoes #2

  1. Prepare 6 of Idaho potatoes.
  2. You need 1/2 C of heavy cream.
  3. Prepare 1/2 C of shredded aged New York cheddar.
  4. You need 2 T of dill butter (see my recipes).
  5. You need 1 T of sour cream.
  6. You need 1/4 t of fresh thyme; minced.
  7. Prepare 1/4 t of lemon peel seasoning.
  8. It's 1/4 t of caraway seeds.
  9. Prepare 1/4 t of garlic powder.
  10. It's 1/4 t of onion powder.
  11. Prepare 1 of large pinch kosher salt and black pepper.
  12. Prepare of olive oil; as needed.

Twice Baked Potatoes #2 instructions

  1. Pierce each potato several times with a small paring knife. Toss in olive oil. Season with dried spices and thyme. Wrap in foil. Bake at 375째F for approximately 1 hour or until potatoes are easily pierced with a paring knife..
  2. When potatoes cool, scoop out pulp into a small mixing bowl. Add dill butter, cream, cheddar, salt, and black pepper. Whip until smooth with a mixer or smash with a potato smasher..
  3. Stuff each potato skin with mashed potato mixture. Bake at 375째F for approximately 10-15 minutes or until mashed potato mixture is thoroughly heated..
