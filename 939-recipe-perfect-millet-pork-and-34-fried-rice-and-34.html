Millet Pork "Fried Rice".

You can have Millet Pork "Fried Rice" using 15 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Millet Pork "Fried Rice"

  1. It's 1 1/2 tsp of sesame pil.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of reduced sodium soy sauce.
  3. It's 1 cup of chopped yellow onion.
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of shredded Brussels sprouts (about 10-12).
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of peanut oil (olive oil works too, if allergies).
  6. It's 2 cloves of garlic, chopoed.
  7. It's 2 tsp of chopped ginger (I'm guilty of using the paste).
  8. Prepare 8 oz of lean ground pork.
  9. Prepare 3/4 tsp of Chinese Five Spice powder.
  10. You need 1/4 tsp of black pepper.
  11. You need 1 of egg plus 2 egg whites, lightly beaten.
  12. It's 3/4 c of millet, toasted and prepared with 2 1/4 c chicken broth.
  13. It's of (can substitute gluten free brown basmati for similar results).
  14. Prepare 1 c of frozen peas, thawed.
  15. You need 1 tbsp of black sesame seeds (I use black & tan gomasio).

Millet Pork "Fried Rice" step by step

  1. Whisk together sesame oil and soy sauce - set adide.
  2. In a medium skillet, sauté onion and sprouts in peanut oil for 3-5 minutes until onion starts to become translucent. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 30 seconds more. Transfer to a large bowl..
  3. Add pork, Chinese powder, and pepper to skillet. Cook until meat is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes. Transfer to the bowl..
  4. Add egg to the skillet and scramble, about 2 minutes. Add to bowl..
  5. Combine millet with the vegetables, pork, and egg, and add the peas. Drizzle with soy mixture and sesame seeds and toss to mix..
