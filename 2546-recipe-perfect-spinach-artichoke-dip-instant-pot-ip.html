Ingredients of Spinach artichoke dip instant pot ip

  1. You need 2 TBSP of salted butter.
  2. It's 1 of yellow onion diced.
  3. You need 3 TBSP of minced garlic.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of chicken broth.
  5. Prepare 14 oz of can artichoke hearts drained.
  6. Prepare 2 TSP of crushed red pepper.
  7. You need 8 oz of baby spinach.
  8. You need 8 oz of cream cheese cubed.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  11. Prepare 2 cups of parmesan cheese.
  12. You need 2 cups of mozzarella cheese.
  13. You need 4 TBSP of real bacon bits.

Easy Instant Pot Spinach Artichoke Dip - the easiest and most delicious appetizer ever! Plus, it's gluten-free, low-carb, and keto-friendly! Tips for Spinach Artichoke Dip in the Instant Pot. Follow directions and be sure to add ingredients in the order stated, DO NOT STIR.

Spinach artichoke dip instant pot ip instructions

  1. Turn on instant pot saute high setting. When pot reads hot add butter..
  2. When butter has melted add onion and cook stirring occasionally for 5 minutes. Then add in garlic and cook for 2 minutes again stirring occasionally..
  3. Add in chicken broth and stir scaping the bottom to remove any bits..
  4. Add in artichokes and give a quick stir..
  5. Add in spinach. Do not stir..
  6. Add cream cheese on top. Do not stir..
  7. Seal instant pot and cook high pressure for 5 minutes. When timer sounds quick release the pressure. Remove lid..
  8. Stir untill cream cheese has melted. Stir in sour cream and mayonnaise..
  9. Stir in parmesan cheese and mozzarella cheese untill melted..
  10. Stir in bacon bits..
  11. Mixture will thicken as it stands..
  12. Enjoy!!.
