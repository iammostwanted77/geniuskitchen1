Ingredients of Vegetarian french onion soup

  1. Prepare 5-6 of White Onion.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of butter.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of oil.
  4. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of black pepper powder.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of Maida.
  7. It's 2/3 cup of Apple Cider Vinegar.
  8. Prepare 1 litre of Veg stock.
  9. You need 2-3 of bay leaves,or bundle of herb(thyme, rosemary and bay leaf).
  10. It's 4 of thick slices of Garlic Bread.
  11. Prepare of Morzzrella Cheese as required.
  12. It's of Processed Cheese as required.

Vegetarian french onion soup step by step

  1. Cut White Onion into Halves.Slice them with the help of slicer.Keep aside..
  2. In heavy bottom non stick pan..add Butter..melt it.. add oil..and also add sliced onions..Mix well,add Salt & Black Pepper powder..
  3. Saute on Medium flame for 30mins.. continuously..till Onions are finely Caramelized...to nice Brown colour.Add Maida..Saute.fir two minutes...to see no lumps formed...
  4. Now add Apple Cider Vinegar..again Saute for few minutes..Add Veg stock..and Bay leaves or bundle of mix herbs..
  5. Let the soup Simmer for 20 mins till thick..on Medium flame..
  6. To ASSEMBLE. VEGETARIAN FRENCH ONION SOUP.(1-Serving).
  7. In a Oven proof Soup Bowl..add 2 laddleful of Prepared Onion Soup..
  8. On it place Thick slice of Toasted & Buttered Garlic Bread.(Cut thick slices from Garlic Bread loaf. apply butter on one side..toast in Airfryer for 3-4mins at180C orToast on Griddle on both sides).
  9. Now grate loads of both.. Morzzrella Cheese and Processed Cheese..on it..
  10. Bake in Preheated Oven..till cheese melt in 180C for 10-15mins..
  11. Relish & serve hot vegetarian french onion soup..
