Baked Sweet Potato Fries with Smoked Paprika step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 450F/230C..
  2. Scrub the the skin of the sweet potatoes very well. Cut into French fry sized matchsticks..
  3. Put in a large bowl and toss the potatoes with spices, salt and oil. Lay out in one layer on a large baking sheet..
  4. Bake for 30-40 minutes. After 15 minutes, give the fries a stir so they get baked evenly on all sides. Bake until slightly browned and crisp in spots..
  5. Serve, sprinkle with salt and pepper if needed. I like to dip these in yogurt or aioli!!.
