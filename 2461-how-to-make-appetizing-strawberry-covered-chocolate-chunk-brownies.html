Ingredients of Strawberry covered chocolate chunk brownies

  5. You need 2 of eggs.

  7. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of dark chocolate chunks.
  9. You need of For layering: 1 cup dark chocolate.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of heavy cream.
  11. It's 8-10 of fresh strawberries.

Strawberry covered chocolate chunk brownies instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 170°C and grease the square tin wuth some oil or butter and line with a parchment paper..
  2. In a bowl sift together all purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking soda and mix it well..
  3. In another bowl take butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract and beat for a minute or till it's light and fluffy..
  4. Next add eggs, one at a time and beat for few seconds after every addition..
  5. Now add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients in batches and mix till well combined and form a smooth lump free batter..
  6. At last add chunks of dark chocolate and mix well..
  7. Pour the brownie batter in greased tin and bake at 170°C for 30-35 minutes..
  8. Once done check with a help of a toothpick, if it comes out clean the brownie is perfectly baked..
  9. Now for layering in a bowl take chopped dark chocolate..
  10. Heat half a cup of heavy cream in a pan for few seconds till it is slightly warm..
  11. Pour the warm cream over chopped dark chocolate and leave it undistured for 5 minutes..
  12. Mix well till smooth and well combined..
  13. Now chop strawberries in small pieces..
  14. Spread chopped strawberries all over the brownie..
  15. Pour the prepared ganache from top and cover the strawberries..
  16. Keep it in refrigerator for 5 minutes so the top layer is nicely set..
  17. Take it out cut in pieces and serve delicious strawberry covered chocolate chunk brownies..
