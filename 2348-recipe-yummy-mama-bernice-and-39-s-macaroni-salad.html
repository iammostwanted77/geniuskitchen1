Ingredients of Mama Bernice's Macaroni Salad

  1. It's 1 box of elbow macaroni.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of Helman's Mayo(to start add more or less depending on your taste).
  3. You need 1 jar of sweet pickles diced.
  4. You need 1 medium of sweet onion diced (eyeball may not use all).
  5. It's 2 of stalks celery diced.
  6. You need 12 of hard boiled eggs.
  7. It's 1 dash of parsley flakes.

Mama Bernice's Macaroni Salad step by step

  1. Boil water cook macaroni per box instructions..
  2. drain and rinse macaroni under cool water. put in lrg tupperware..
  3. boil water add eggs cook approx 12min.Drain rinse under cold water. set in fridge until ready to use..
  4. add celery;onion,sweet pickles,and mayo fold into macaroni.
  5. de-shell eggs do a quik dice add to bowl.
  6. add a splash of pickle juice(and more mayo depending on your taste).
  7. add dash parsley flakes. mix well. refridgerate at least an hour before serving. it does taste better next day ..
