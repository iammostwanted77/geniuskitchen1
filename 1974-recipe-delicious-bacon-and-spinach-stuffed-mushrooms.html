Ingredients of Bacon and Spinach stuffed mushrooms

  2. Prepare 1 package of stuffing mix.
  3. Prepare 1 package of bacon.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of minced garlic.
  5. You need 1 package (10 oz) of chopped spinach.
  6. You need of olive oil.
  7. Prepare 40 of fresh mushrooms.
  8. It's 2 cups of shredded cheese parmesan and mozzarella blend.

Bacon and Spinach stuffed mushrooms step by step

  1. Heat oven to 400°F.
  2. Prepare stuffing mix as directed on package.
  3. Pan fry bacon until crisp.
  4. Remove stems from mushrooms.
  5. Chop stems and bacon together.
  6. Saute mushroom stems and bacon together with garlic until tender.
  7. Mix together mushroom and bacon mixture along with the stuffing spinach and cheese.
  8. Spoon into mushroom caps place filled sides up and top with cheese.
  9. Bake 20 to 25 minutes until cheese is golden brown.
