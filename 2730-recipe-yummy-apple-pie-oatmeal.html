Recipe: Yummy Apple Pie Oatmeal

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Apple Pie Oatmeal.

You can have Apple Pie Oatmeal using 12 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Apple Pie Oatmeal

  1. You need 1/2 cup of oatmeal.
  2. You need 1 cup of milk (could be more).
  3. It's 1/2 of apple (cut into dice).
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of raisins (I used golden raisins and less than 1/4 cup).
  5. You need 1/4 cup of pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds (I used less than it).
  6. It's 7 of almonds (chopped).
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of ground nutmeg.
  9. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  10. You need 1 pinch of ground cloves (optional).
  11. You need 1/2 tbs of honey.
  12. It's 1/2 tbs of brown sugar.

Apple Pie Oatmeal step by step

  1. Boil in medium low heat all the ingredients (except honey), stir them all and add more liquid such as milk or water if needed to reach the consistency that you want..
  2. Simmer around 5 minutes (could be less or more) or until the apple a bit soften..
  3. Garnish with ground cinnamon and a lil bit drizzles of honey (if you use honey for glazing the top, make sure to reduce the brown sugar).
