Ingredients of IPA Crab Cakes with Spicy Beer Hollandaise

  1. It's of Crab Cakes.
  2. You need 8 oz of fresh lump crab.
  3. You need 1 of egg.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of green onions (chopped).
  5. You need 1/4 cup of roasted red peppers (chopped).
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of old bay seasoning.
  9. It's 1 pinch of cayenne pepper.
  10. You need 2 tbsp of IPA beer.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of butter (melted).
  12. Prepare 1 cup of panko crumbs plus 1 additional cup divided.
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup of canola or vegetable oil.
  14. You need of Spicy Beer Hollandaise.
  15. It's 4 tbsp of butter.
  16. Prepare 5 of egg yolks.
  17. It's 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  18. It's 2 tbsp of IPA Beer.
  19. You need 1/2 tsp of Sriracha sauce.
  20. You need 1 of salt and pepper.

IPA Crab Cakes with Spicy Beer Hollandaise step by step

  1. In a medium sized bowl, mix together all crab cake ingredients plus one cup of panko bread crumbs..
  2. Form into 4 patties about one inch thick..
  3. Place remaining panko in a small bowl. One at a time, place the patties into the bowl until coated on all sides..
  4. In a pan over medium high heat, add the canola oil. Cook crab cakes until golden brown on the underside. Flip carefully and cook until cooked through, about 3 minutes on each side..
  5. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels..
  6. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels..
  7. Place 4 tablespoons of butter in a microwavable glass dish..
  8. Microwave on high for 45 seconds or until melted. Remove and let stand for one minute. Skim froth from the top..
  9. In a large saucepan add lemon, egg yolks and beer..
  10. Whisk quickly and continually over low heat until mixture is frothy and doubled in size. About 5 minutes. Keep heat low or eggs will scramble..
  11. While continuously whisking, stream in the melted butter, continue whisking until thickened. About 5 minutes..
  12. Add in chili sauce and salt and pepper to taste..
  13. Drizzle sauce over crab cakes and serve..
