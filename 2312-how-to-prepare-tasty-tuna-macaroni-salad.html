  1. You need 2 cup of elbow noodles.
  2. Prepare 2 of hard boiled eggs (chopped).
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of chopped celery.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of chopped green peppers.
  5. You need 2 of chopped scallions.
  6. Prepare 6 slice of of cucumber chopped.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of fresh chopped parsley.
  8. You need 1 can of flaked tuna in water drained.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of shredded carrot.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  11. You need 1/4 cup of ranch dressing.

tuna macaroni salad step by step

  1. Boil noodles in salted water with oil and also boil the eggs and put aside.
  2. Chop up the eggs..celery..green pepper..scallion..cucumber..parsley and add the shredded carrots and tuna along with the noodles and mix all together.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix together ...chill for a bit and serve !.
