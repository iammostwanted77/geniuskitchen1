Ingredients of Spicy Sweet Potato Fries

  2. It's 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. You need 1/2 tsp of chilli powder.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of ground cumin.
  5. You need 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper.

Spicy Sweet Potato Fries step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 200c.
  2. Scrub the the skin of the potatoes well and cut off any bad bits, before cutting into chunky chips.
  3. Use kitchen towel to dry the potatoes / absorb any water.
  4. Place in a large bowl and toss the potatoes with spices and oil. Lay out in one layer on a large baking tray.
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes. After 15 minutes, give the fries a stir so they get baked evenly on all sides. Bake until slightly browned and crisp in spots..
