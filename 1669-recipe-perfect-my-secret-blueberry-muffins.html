Ingredients of My Secret Blueberry Muffins

  1. It's 50 grams of Butter (or margarine).
  2. Prepare 40 grams of Granulated sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 of Egg.
  4. You need 40 grams of Milk.
  5. You need 80 grams of ★Flour.
  6. You need 3 grams of ★Baking powder.
  7. You need 50 grams of Blueberries (frozen).

My Secret Blueberry Muffins step by step

  1. Sift together the ★ ingredients. Bring butter, egg and milk to room temperature..
  2. In a bowl, add butter and beat until soft. Add sugar little by little and beat until fluffy..
  3. Slowly add the beaten egg and whisk well..
  4. Add flour in three batches, alternating with milk (flour-milk-flour-milk-flour.) Combine lightly using a rubber spatula. Add the blueberries..
  5. Spoon the batter into the muffin cups and top with more blueberries if desired. Bake in a 180℃ oven for 25~30 minutes..
  6. You can definitely use fresh blueberries. Raspberries may be good also. If you don't have berries, make plain muffins..
