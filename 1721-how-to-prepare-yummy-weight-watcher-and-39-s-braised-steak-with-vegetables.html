Ingredients of Weight Watcher's Braised Steak with Vegetables

  1. Prepare 4 of (4 oz )cubed steaks.
  2. You need 2 of onions, sliced.
  3. Prepare 1 can of no salt- stewed tomatoes.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of dry red wine.
  5. It's 3 of carrots, thinly sliced.
  6. You need 2 cup of sliced mushrooms.
  7. Prepare 2 of bay leaves.
  8. Prepare 2 of bay leaves.
  9. You need 2 medium of zucchini, sliced.
  10. It's 2 tbsp of all purpose flour.

Weight Watcher's Braised Steak with Vegetables step by step

  1. Spray large saucepan with PAM and brown steaks about 2 minutes on each side, transfer to a plate.
  2. Spray same pan again and saute onions until softened. About 5 minutes..
  3. Add flour, stirring constantly, until flour is lightly browned. About 1 minute.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and wine.
  5. Add carrots, mushrooms, bay leaves, pepper and steaks. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, about 30- 40 minutes.
  7. Add zuchinni, return to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered 5- 6 minutes.
  8. Serve steaks topped with vegetable mixture.
  9. WW points= 5. 293 calories.
  10. Serves 4.
