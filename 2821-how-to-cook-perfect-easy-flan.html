Ingredients of Easy Flan

  1. It's 500 ml of Milk.
  2. You need 4 of Eggs.
  3. Prepare 125 grams of Sugar.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Vanilla extract.
  5. You need of caramel.
  6. Prepare 150 grams of Sugar.

Easy Flan instructions

  1. In a pan melt the sugar until its a caramel golden brown.
  2. Pour the caramel into the flan mold.
  3. Into a bowl mix eggs, milk, sugar and vanilla, until well mixed, you can use a hand mixer.
  4. Pour the mix into the flan mold and take to the oven in "baño María" por about 1 hour at a moderate temperature.
