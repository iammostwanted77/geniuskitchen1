Ingredients of Gumbo Casserole

  1. Prepare 2-10 3/4 oz of cans Campbell's Condensed Chicken Gumbo Soup.
  2. It's 1 of -Soup can full of water.
  3. Prepare 1 of -teaspoon dried minced onion.
  4. You need 1/2 of -teaspoon Cajun seasoning.
  5. Prepare 1/2 of -teaspoon garlic powder.
  6. You need 3/4 of -cup uncooked instant white rice.
  7. It's 1/2 pound of cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined.

Gumbo (French: Gombo) is a soup popular in the U. S. state of Louisiana, and is the official state cuisine. Gumbo consists primarily of a strongly-flavored stock, meat or shellfish, a thickener. A traditional roux based gumbo made with shrimp, spicy andouille sausage and okra.

Gumbo Casserole step by step

  1. Stir the soup, water, onion, Cajun seasoning, garlic powder, rice, and shrimp in 2-quart casserole dish.
  2. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes or until hot..
  3. Stir before serving.
