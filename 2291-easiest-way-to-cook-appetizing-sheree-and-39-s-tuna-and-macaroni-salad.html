Ingredients of Sheree's Tuna and Macaroni Salad

  1. Prepare 2 can of tuna (I like mine packed in oil).
  2. It's 1 can of peas.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of celery.
  4. You need 1 envelope of dry ranch dressing mix.
  5. It's 1 cup of mayonnise.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  7. You need 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Prepare 8 oz of uncooked macaroni.

Sheree's Tuna and Macaroni Salad instructions

  1. cook macaroni noodles according to package. rinse under cold water so that they stop cooking.....
  2. in large bowl add macaroni, celery, tuna, and peas. make sure tuna is broke up..
  3. in another bowl mix mayonnaise, dressing mix, and milk very well..
  4. stir mayonnaise mixture into tuna mixture..
  5. add salt and pepper to taste..
  6. cover and refrigerate until your ready to serve..
