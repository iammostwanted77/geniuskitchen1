Cold Ranch Macaroni Salad step by step

  1. Cook noodles in pan until soft drain and let sit to cool..
  2. Mince onion and peppers..
  3. Tear apart salami, turkey and pepperoni to decently sized pieces about half an inch..
  4. Drain black olives and combine all in a bowl at once..
  5. Stir all ingredients together and then add your ranch. Depending on your preference with ranch I'd suggest starting out with half a cup and if that doesn't satisfy you then I'd add up to 2 cups..
  6. Add shred cheese..
  7. optional: add the kraft macaroni cheese packet if you'd like, but I don't recommend in doing so...it tastes to grainy..
  8. After all is combinded set in fridge to cool to a cold temperature. I would make this around 12 noon if you want this for dinner. Make sure to cover your creation. :).
