Ingredients of FRENCH ONION SOUP

  7. It's 1 dash of PEPPER TO TASTE.
  8. You need 1 dash of Salt to taste (keep in mind the sodium in the broth and cheese).
  9. You need 2 cup of Swiss & or Gruyer Cheese shredded.

FRENCH ONION SOUP step by step

  1. Melt butter in a large pot..
  2. Saute onions till tender, keeping a lid on. 10-15 min..
  3. Remove lid & allow onions to become golden brown. 10-20 min. Stir often..
  4. Add wine scrapping bits off bottom of pot..
  5. Add broth, worcestershire sauce, pepper to taste..
  6. Turn down to low and simmer until broth has reduced to half or to your liking. Approx. 30 min..
  7. Toast French bread on both sides. To assemble fill your soup crock (oven safe) place bread on top of soup & generously top with cheese & broil until cheese is golden..
  8. ENJOY :).
