Pineapple upside down cake.

You can cook Pineapple upside down cake using 15 ingredients and 12 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Pineapple upside down cake

  1. Prepare of For the cake.
  2. You need 150 gms of All purpose flour.
  3. It's 150 gms of powdered sugar.
  4. You need 150 gms of butter.
  5. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  6. It's of vanilla essence.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of baking powdered.
  8. It's of quarter cup chopped pineapple.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  10. Prepare 2-3 tbsp of milk.
  11. Prepare of For the base.
  12. You need 6-7 of pineapple slices & cored.
  13. Prepare 10-12 of glazed cherries.
  14. Prepare 3-4 tbsp of butter.
  15. It's 3-4 tbsp of brown sugar/ white sugar also can be used.

Pineapple upside down cake instructions

  1. In a baking dish, melt 3-4 tbsp butter and sprinkle brown sugar/ white sugar over the butter..
  2. Place the pineapple slices over the butter and sugar mixture. Place glazed cherries in the center of each pineapple slice and where ever there are gaps. Set aside..
  3. For the cake. Preheat an oven at 200 degree for 10 mins..
  4. Sieve flour, baking powdered, baking soda and a pinch of salt together and set aside..
  5. In a mixing bowl, cream the butter & sugar till light & fluffy..
  6. Add the eggs one at a times. Adding 1 spoon of flour for each egg. Whisk. Add vanilla essence & mix well..
  7. Now add the flour using cut & fold method..
  8. If the batter is too thick at this stage you can add milk..
  9. Once the batter is well mixed fold it the chopped pineapple pieces..
  10. Once the batter is ready, transfer to the baking dish with the prepared pineapple slices & bake for 40-45 mins at 150 degree or depending on the oven..
  11. Remove for oven and cool on wire rack. Once cooled turn upside down..
  12. Cake and serve. This cake taste best with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Enjoyyy.
