Lemon Chicken with Capers on Wheat Pasta instructions

  1. I like to grill my chicken with salt, pepper, garlic salt and butter. Grill 4 to 6 minutes on each side and then place in a dish and set aside..
  2. In a skillet on medium high melt butter and add diced garlic for 1 to 2 minutes. Then add white wine, chicken broth, and lemon juice. Place chicken breast in sauce. Add capers, cover and simmer 10 to 15 minutes..
  3. Add to angle hair pasta or wheat pasta and enjoy!.
