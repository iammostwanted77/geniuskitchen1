Ingredients of Quiche

  4. It's 6 strips of cook Bacon.
  5. Prepare 4 of eggs separated.
  6. You need Dash of salt.
  7. It's Dash of pepper.
  8. Prepare 4 tablespoon of cream cheese.
  9. You need 1/4 of shredded jack cheese.
  10. Prepare 9 inch of deep dish pie crust.

Quiche step by step

  1. Cook mushroom green onions till it soft add the spinach cook for extra minute.
  2. Separate the eggs.
  3. Beat the eggs yolk with the cream cheese for 2 minutes then add the mushrooms mixture add black pepper..
  4. Fluffy the egg white with little bit of the salt.
  5. Spread the 1/4 shredded jack cheese on pie crust shredded the bacon.
  6. Fold the egg white with egg yolk mixture don't over mix..
  7. Add the eggs mixture on top jack cheese and bacon..
  8. Cook at reheated oven at 350 for 30 minutes..
  9. Enough for 4 people.
  10. Easy to cut.
