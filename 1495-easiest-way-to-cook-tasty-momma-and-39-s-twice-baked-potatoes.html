Ingredients of Momma's twice baked potatoes

  1. Prepare 4 large of potatoes.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  3. Prepare 1/2 packages of bacon.
  4. It's 2 cup of cheese.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of garlic powder.
  6. You need 2 tsp of black pepper.
  7. It's 1 dash of salt.

Momma's twice baked potatoes instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. poke potatoes with a fork.
  3. bake potatoes until tender all the way through.
  4. take potatoes and let them cool.
  5. cut tops out of potatoes.
  6. scoop insides out leaving the skin in tact.
  7. mix potato insides, chesse, sour cream, bacon, salt, pepper, and garlic in a mixing bowl.
  8. spoon mixture back into potato skins.
  9. bake again at 350 for about an hour or until tops get golden brown.
