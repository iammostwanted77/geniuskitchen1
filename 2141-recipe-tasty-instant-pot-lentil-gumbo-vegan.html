Ingredients of Instant pot lentil gumbo vegan

  1. It's 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. It's 1 tsp of minced garlic or 2 cloves.
  3. Prepare 1 of red bell Pepper (1 c chopped).
  4. Prepare 1.5 c of chopped onion.
  5. You need 2 of celery ribs (1 cup chopped).
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of fresh thyme or 1 tsp dried.
  7. It's 1/2 tbsp of fresh oregano or 1/2 tsp dried.
  8. Prepare 1/2-1 tsp of Cajun mix spice (the more the spicier).
  9. It's 1/2 tsp of cayenne.
  10. You need 1 c of lentils.
  11. You need 3 cups of vegetable broth or chicken broth.
  12. Prepare 2 cups of fresh or frozen chopped okra.
  13. You need 1 can of salt free diced tomatoes (13 ounces)- slightly drained.
  14. It's 2 tbsp of Apple Cider Vinegar.
  15. It's 1/2 c of tomato sauce- salt free if possible.
  16. You need of Optional 1 c cauliflower - riced or finely chopped.
  17. It's to taste of Kosher Salt or sea salt and pepper.
  18. Prepare of sliced jalapeño and fresh cilantro to garnish.

Instant pot lentil gumbo vegan instructions

  1. Sauté oil, onion, garlic, bell pepper, celery for 5’minutes on sauté setting, until softened and fragrant..
  2. Add spices and mix again for 1 minute..
  3. Add remaining ingredients (minus salt and pepper). Mix together. Place lid on pressure cooker then place setting on pressure cook high for 12 minutes..
  4. After cooking. Add 1/2 tsp to 1 tsp sea salt or kosher salt to taste. Black Pepper to taste Stir and Keep warm. Important: Don't add extra salt to lentil gumbo while pressure cooking because it can ruin ruin texture of lentils..
  5. Once ready to serve into bowls, garnish with red pepper flakes, jalapeños, and fresh cilantro. Since the cauliflower rice is added to the instant pot, you don't need to serve this over rice. See notes for other options..
