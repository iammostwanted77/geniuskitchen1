Ingredients of Cheese Flan ( Puertorrican Flan)

  1. Prepare 1 can of Evaporated milk.
  2. It's 1 can of Condense Milk.
  3. You need 8 oz of cream cheese.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  5. It's 1 dash of canela.
  6. It's 3 large of eggs.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of sugar.
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup of water.

Cheese Flan ( Puertorrican Flan) step by step

  1. Preheat oven at 350°f.
  2. Cook 1 cup sugar in a medium-size saucepan over medium heat, and cook, stirring constantly, 5 minutes or until sugar melts and turns a light golden brown. Quickly pour hot caramelized sugar into a 2-quart flan dish. Using oven mitts, tilt dish to evenly coat bottom and sides. Let stand 5 minutes. Sugar will harden..
  3. In a blender add the milk, condense milk and eggs, mix well and add the cheese and remaining ingredients, blend well..
  4. Bake in (bano de maria) mari bath, for 1 hour or until knife comes out clean..
  5. Let it cool overnight, enjoy!.
