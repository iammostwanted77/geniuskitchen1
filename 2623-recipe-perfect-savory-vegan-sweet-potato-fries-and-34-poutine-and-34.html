Ingredients of Savory Vegan Sweet Potato Fries "Poutine"

  1. Prepare 2 tbsp of vegan butter (plus more to make smooth).
  2. Prepare 1/8 cup of flour.
  3. It's 1 cup of vegetable broth.
  4. It's 1-2 tsp of soy sauce.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of thyme.
  6. It's 1/4 tsp of sage.
  7. It's 1/4 tsp of nutmeg.
  8. You need 2 tsp of garlic powder.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of vegan mozzarella cheese.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of vegan protein crumbles.
  11. Prepare 1/2 package of sweet potato fries.

Savory Vegan Sweet Potato Fries "Poutine" step by step

  1. Cook sweet potato fries according to the product packaging around the prep and cooking for gravy sauce..
  2. Melt vegan butter in medium saucepan or fry pan over medium-low. Add flour and stir til smooth. Add more butter to make smooth if necessary..
  3. Add vegetable broth to flour mixture and stir til smooth..
  4. Add soy sauce (more or less to taste)..
  5. Add (4) dry spices and stir..
  6. Pour cheese and protein crumbles on top of gravy sauce. Cover pot or pan to speed up the melting process without burning. Let melt for 5-10 minutes (depending on how melty you want the cheese - the less time, the more poutine-like)..
  7. Stir one final time and serve over sweet potato fries..
