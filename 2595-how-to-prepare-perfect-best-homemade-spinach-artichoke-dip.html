BEST Homemade Spinach Artichoke Dip.

You can have BEST Homemade Spinach Artichoke Dip using 13 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of BEST Homemade Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. It's 2 cups of chopped up Artichoke Hearts.
  2. You need 2 cup of chopped up fresh spinach (frozen to thaw is best) Make sure to squeeze out the excess liquid.
  3. It's 3 of heaping tablespoons of garlic, minced (Garlic mince in a jar is best).
  4. It's 1 of whole onion.
  5. It's 1 of full cup of sour cream.
  6. You need 1 package (8 oz.) of softened cream cheese.
  7. It's 1 of full cup of Dukes or Hellmann's Mayo.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of milk.
  9. Prepare 1 of heaping handful of shredded mozzarella cheese and 1 heaping handful of mild cheddar cheese (plus save for sprinkling on top).
  10. You need 1 Handful of grated Parmesan cheese.
  11. Prepare 1 Stick of Real Butter.
  12. It's 1 Tablespoon of Italian Seasoning.
  13. It's of Add Salt and Pepper.

BEST Homemade Spinach Artichoke Dip step by step

  1. First you want to saute your onions along with your garlic and Italian Seasoning all in one pan. Next melt in your stick of butter, cream cheese and milk with a whisk until the cream cheese is completely melted and incorporated. Cook on low-medium heat so it doesn't burn..
  2. Preheat the oven to 400. Now Combine all your sauteed ingredients in bowl and mix in your spinach, artichokes, sour cream, mayo, and all your cheeses. Combine it all together. Now place in a baking dish and add remaining mozzarella and mild cheddar on top or just mix the cheeses together and add on top of the dip for baking. Bake for at least 30 minutes until bubbly and the cheese has a nice light golden brown..
  3. OPTIONAL: You can always mix in green chiles, hot pepper flakes, and pimentos for extra flavor and kick..
  4. Want some Homemade Tortilla Chips? Buy a pack of floured Tortilla's and stack 3 or 4 together and cut into 4's. Now use 100% Crisco Vegetable oil or Pure Crisco in a safe pot to fry in or use a fryer. Add in your tortilla's whichever amount can fit and stir around in the oil using tongs until a perfectly light brown. Remove from oil, (use paper towel to tab excess oil off). Sprinkle with Parmesan Cheese and serve for your guests or just yourself. :).
