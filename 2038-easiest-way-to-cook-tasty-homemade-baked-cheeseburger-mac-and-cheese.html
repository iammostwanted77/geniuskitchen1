Ingredients of Homemade Baked Cheeseburger Mac and Cheese

  1. It's of Cheeseburger Sauce.
  2. It's 1 lb of Ground Beef (or your choice of ground meat).
  3. It's 2 box of Velveeta Cheese (Room temp or pre-melted [microwaved]).
  4. You need 8 dash of Worcestershire Sauce.
  5. You need 5 tbsp of Ketchup.
  6. Prepare 5 tbsp of Mustard (I used Spicy Brown).
  7. You need 10 dash of Montreal Steak Seasoning (Do this more to taste).
  8. You need of Baked Part.
  9. You need 1 lb of Bag of Elbow Macaroni.
  10. Prepare 3/4 cup of Shredded Cheese (Your Choice... I used Mexican Blend).
  11. Prepare 3/4 cup of Bread Crumbs.

Homemade Baked Cheeseburger Mac and Cheese step by step

  1. Preheat Oven to 375Â°.
  2. Cook Ground Beef and add in Worcestershire, Ketchup, Mustard, and Montreal Steak Seasoning while its cooking... DO NOT DRAIN!.
  3. While meat is cooking cook the Elbow Macaroni and drain once done... put back in pot so you can mix sauce and macaroni once sauce is done.
  4. While pasta is cooking start adding Velveeta Cheese to beef mixture and allow it to melt and be stirred in to create sauce.
  5. Mix finished sauce and pasta together and place in sprayed or buttered casserole dish.
  6. Sprinkle Shredded Cheese to your liking and then add Bread Crumbs to your liking as well.
  7. Place in oven for about 20 - 30 mins or until top is Golden to light brown (DO NOT COVER WITH FOIL).
  8. Suggestions: Cut it to the portion sizes you want once it comes out of oven and then let it cool off for about 2 mins... I used Cooking Spray at the end to spray the Bread Crumbs to make them crisp up better... Also to minimize time try to boil the water as the ground beef is cooking (common sense, I know but some people might not know)... serve with salad for a more balanced meal.
