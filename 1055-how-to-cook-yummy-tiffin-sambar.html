Tiffin sambar step by step

  1. Cook toor dal with turmeric powder and water in a pressure cooker.
  2. Grind all ingredients mentioned under 'to grind'.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add all the ingredients mentioned under 'to temper'. Add small onions and chopped carrot. Cook for a while. Add cooked dal and cook for few minutes. Then add the ground paste and finally tamarind pulp. Make it to boil. Add required salt. And finally garnish with coriander leaves..
