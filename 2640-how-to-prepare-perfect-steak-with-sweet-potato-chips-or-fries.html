Steak with sweet potato chips (or fries) step by step

  1. Heat oven to 390°F.
  2. Finely chop parsley and tarragon.
  3. Peel and slice sweet potatoes with mandolin. Alternatively, cut it to thin French fries.
  4. Put sliced sweet potato (use mandolin) on baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil and seasoning. Cook 20 minutes until edges are golden brown.
  5. Put shallots and vinegar in bowl and leave for 5 minutes.
  6. Add nuts, tarragon, 1/2 parsley, some salt, and 1 1/2 tsp olive oil to bowl.
  7. Oil and season steak fillets.
  8. Griddle or pan fry steak 3 minutes for medium rare. Rest for 5 minutes in foil.
  9. When chips are done, sprinkle with remaining parsley, salt, and any seasoning.
  10. Enjoy with green salad.
