Twice Baked Loaded Potato Skins instructions

  1. Wash and dry potatoes. Prick with fork a few times to allow steam to escape..
  2. Rub oil over potatoes to cover completely..
  3. Place potatoes on oven rack and bake at 400 until tender in center..
  4. Slice potatoes lengthwise. Scoop out potato leaving small amount to help sturdy the sides of skins..
  5. Place skins back in oven to crisp up. About 10 minutes..
  6. Meanwhile mash potatoes with butter, chives, cheese, sour cream, bacon bits salt and pepper..
  7. Mix until creamy. Remove skins from oven and scoop filling into skins evenly..
  8. Bake until tops of potatoes are golden brown..
  9. Add additional cheese if you like..
  10. You can add any seasonings you like on your potato mixture. I just love the bacon and chives and cheese..
  11. Hope you enjoy.
