vegan gumbo step by step

  1. in a saucepan, combine flour and oil and stir over medium heat until mixture turns a light brown.
  2. pour the mixture into a large pot over medium heat.
  3. add tomatoes to the pot and let simmer for awhile. in the same saucepan, fry chicken cutlets and adding salt and pepper. set aside when finished..
  4. add the cans of veggies, undrained to the pot, along with the spices.
  5. simmer covered about a half an hour.
  6. top with cheese when serving.
  7. add chicken cutlets, after cutting them up, and replace cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. top with cheese when serving.
