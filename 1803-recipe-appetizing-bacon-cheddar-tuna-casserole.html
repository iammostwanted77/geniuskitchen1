Ingredients of Bacon Cheddar Tuna Casserole

  1. You need 2 cup of Mini shell pasta, uncooked.
  2. You need 1 cup of Frozen Broccoli.
  3. You need 1 cup of Frozen peas and carrots mix.
  4. Prepare 1 stick of Butter, salted.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of Flour.
  6. It's 2 cup of Milk.
  7. It's 2 cup of Cheddar cheese, shredded.
  8. You need 1 can of Bacon crumbles (I use Kirkland brand).
  9. It's 1 can of Albacore tuna in water (7 ounces).
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of Crushed plain potato chips.
