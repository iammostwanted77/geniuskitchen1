Ingredients of Quiche

  1. Prepare 9 of -inch pie crust.
  2. You need 3 of eggs.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of milk.
  5. You need 1 cup of grated gruyere.
  6. It's 5 slices of Applegate ham or turkey, sliced into bites.
  7. It's 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  8. You need 1/2 teaspoon of pepper.

Quiche instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 410 degrees..
  2. Line a 9-inch pie plate with the pastry. Build a rim with the dough and flute it. Poke bottom with holes..
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Stir in ham and cheese..
  4. Pour filling into pie crust, set on a baking sheet and bake for 40 minutes, or until set..
