Broc, Mozz, bac quiche instructions

  1. Lay out pie crust in pie pan or buy an already put together pie crust..
  2. Whisk 6+ eggs depending on the size of your pan..
  3. Season the egg batter.
  4. Add a little bit of milk into the egg batter and whisk.
  5. Pour egg batter into pie crust pan.
  6. Fry bacon separately when done sprinkle over pie.
  7. Bake broccoli then sprinkle over pie.
  8. Cut your fav cheese as small or big as you'd like and place on pie.
  9. Put quiche to bake for about 15+ mins until risenat 350*.
