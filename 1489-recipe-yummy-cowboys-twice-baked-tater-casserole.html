Ingredients of cowboys twice baked tater casserole

  1. You need 8 each of med. russet taters.
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of butter.
  4. Prepare 8 oz of sour cream.
  5. You need 1 tsp of black pepper.
  6. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  7. It's 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.
  9. You need 3 cup of shredded cheddar cheese divided.
  10. You need 1/2 lb of bacon fried and crumbled.
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of diced green onions.
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp of parsley.

cowboys twice baked tater casserole step by step

  1. prehaet oven to 350°F..
  2. peel, chop, and boil taters until fork tender..
  3. drain taters and return to pot..
  4. cook, drain, and crumble bacon..
  5. dice green onions..
  6. add remaining ingredients (leave out 1 c of cheese) and mix until smooth..
  7. add bacon and green onions. mix well..
  8. spoon taters into greased baking dish..
  9. bake at 350 for 35 to 40 minutes..
  10. top with remaining cheese and garnish with parsley..
