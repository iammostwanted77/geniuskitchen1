Ingredients of Crustless quiche

  1. It's 4 of eggs.
  2. You need 1 cup of milk.
  3. You need 2 spoons of flour.
  4. It's 1 cup of grated cheese.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of grated or finely chopped ham.
  6. You need 1 of medium onion and 2-3 green onions.
  7. Prepare of salt and pepper.

Crustless quiche instructions

  1. Beat the eggs, add the flour and the milk. Add salt and pepper.
  2. In a frying pan put the onion, finely chopped, until transparent, then add the ham and fry until golden..
  3. Add the onion, ham and grated cheese to the eggs, milk and flour mixture and stir very thoroughly together..
  4. Pour in an oiled pan (I used the cake molds with detachable edges) and cook in preheated oven on medium setting for 35-45 minutes..
  5. Remove from oven when it is golden on top..
