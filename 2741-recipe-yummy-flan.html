Flan step by step

  1. Put the eggs and the sugar in a bowl, and mix until the yolks have blended with the whites and the sugar.
  2. Slowly add the milk (cold) to the mix, pouring 1/5 at a time and mixing in between pours.
  3. Add vanilla essence (to taste, I use 10 drops or a teaspoon) and mix.
  4. Pour mix into small containers (50ml to 100ml in size), using a sieve not only to remove the parts that didn’t mix well, but to prevent air bubbles to form in the mix.
  5. Cook in microwave in intervals of 30 seconds, checking for its consistency each time the timer finishes. It should be around 2 min 30 sec total, or 5 cycles, but this varies from microwave to microwave.
  6. Once cooked, let the flan rest 30 minutes over the counter, and then move to the fridge for at least another 30 minutes.
  7. Enjoy with a spoonful of dulce de leche for an Argentine twist.
