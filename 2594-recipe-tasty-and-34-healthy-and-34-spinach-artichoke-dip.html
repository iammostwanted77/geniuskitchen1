Ingredients of "Healthy" Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. You need 10 oz of fresh spinach.
  2. Prepare 6 oz of 1/3 less fat cream cheese.
  3. You need 6 oz of fat free cream cheese.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of fat free sour cream.
  5. You need 1 1/2 cup of part-skim mozzarella shredded.
  6. It's 1 can of artichoke hearts (drained and chopped).
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp of fresh ground black pepper.
  8. You need 3 of garlic cloves crushed.
  9. Prepare 6 tbsp of shredded Parmesan cheese, divided.

"Healthy" Spinach Artichoke Dip step by step

  1. pre-heat oven to 350°F.
  2. tear spinach into bite size pieces removing any thick stems. Rinse in a colander leaving a little water on the leaves. In a large non stick pan (or Dutch oven) saute the spinach over medium heat until wilted. Drain in the colander pressing the extra water out..
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cream cheeses with a potato masher. Add sour cream and mash more. Add spinach, mozzarella, artichokes, pepper, garlic, and 2 tablespoons of the Parmesan. Stir everything until thoroughly combined..
  4. Pour mixture into a 1 1/2 quart baking dish. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup of Parmesan on top. Bake 30 minutes, or until Parmesan is melted dip is all bubbly. Remove from oven and give it a minute or two to cool down. Serve with baked tortilla chips to applause..
