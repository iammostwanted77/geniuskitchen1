Ingredients of Cocunut Topped Blueberry Muffins

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup of softened butter.
  3. It's 2 cup of flour.
  4. It's 1 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt.
  6. It's 1 of egg.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of lolive oil.
  9. It's 1 cup of sugar.
  10. You need 1 cup of blueberries, fresh or frozen.

Cocunut Topped Blueberry Muffins instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°..
  2. Combine sour cream, butter..
  3. Add flour, baking powder, salt cut with fok till crumbly..
  4. Mix together the egg, milk, oil and sugar. Add to the flour mixture and mix just until combine..
  5. Fold in blueberries..
  6. Pour into a greased or paper lined muffin tin 3/4 full..
  7. Top each with a tablespoon or so of coconut..
  8. Bake 25 minutes or until when toothpic inserted comes out clean..
