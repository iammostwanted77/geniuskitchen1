Ingredients of Crockpot Chicken And Sausage Gumbo

  1. Prepare 1/3 cup of Flour.
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup of Cooking oil.
  3. It's 3 cup of Chicken broth.
  4. You need 12 oz of Smoked sausage sliced 1/2" thick.
  5. You need 3 cup of Diced cooked chicken.
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of Sliced okra.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of Chopped onion.
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of Chopped celery.
  9. Prepare 4 clove of Minced garlic.
  10. It's 1 of Salt to taste.
  11. It's 1/2 tsp of Pepper.
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Red pepper.

Crockpot Chicken And Sausage Gumbo step by step

  1. For roux, in a heavy 2-quart saucepan stir together flour and oil until smooth. Cook over medium-high heat for 4 minutes, stirring constantly..
  2. Reduce heat to medium. Continue to cook, stirring constantly, for about 15 minutes more or until roux is dark reddish brown. Let roux cool..
  3. Add chicken broth to a 3 1/2 to 6-quart slow cooker. Stir in roux. Add sausage, chicken, okra, onion, green pepper, celery, garlic, salt, pepper, and red pepper..
  4. Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 10 hours, or HIGH for 4 to 5 hours..
