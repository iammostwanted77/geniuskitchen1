Chocolate Strawberry Shortcakes step by step

  1. Make Whoppie Pie Cakes and cool them as directed in recipe, recipe attached below https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/351994-original-whoppie-pies.
  2. While whoppie pie cakes are cooling , combine strawberrys and sugar and mix well. Let sit to extract juices.
  3. Make Stabalized Whipped Cream, using double of the ingredients following the same directions. Recipe attached below https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/343417-stabilized-whipped-cream-for-frosting-topping-and-dipping.
  4. ASSEMBLE SHORTCAKES.
  5. Put one Whoppie Pie bottom up on serving plate.
  6. Put some whipped cream on cake.
  7. Add some sweetened strawberrys on top of whipped cream.
  8. Top with another whoppie pie cake.
  9. Repeat 1 more time by adding whipped cream, strawberrys and third whoppie pie cake.
  10. Finish top with whipped cream and strawberrys.
