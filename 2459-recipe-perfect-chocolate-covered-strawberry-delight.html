Chocolate covered strawberry delight step by step

  1. Gently rinse strawberries and dry on paper towels (berries must be completely dry). Line cookie sheet with waxed paper..
  2. In double boiler method or microwave for 30 seconds melt chocolate chips and stirring frequently..
  3. Cut strawberries base for stand Dip lower half of each strawberry into chocolate mixture; allow excess to drip back into saucepan. Place on waxed paper-lined cookie sheet..
  4. Refrigerate uncovered about 30 minutes or until chocolate is firm, than garnish with white whipped cream than refrigerate ready to serve..
  5. When served plating with orange slices and pomegranate seeds arrange and place strawberries roses on the top of chocolate covered strawberries. and enjoy.
