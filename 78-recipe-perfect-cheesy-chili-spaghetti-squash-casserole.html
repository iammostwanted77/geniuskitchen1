Cheesy chili spaghetti squash casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Cut open raw squash lengthwise, steam or roast for an hour. When ready, remove 5C of strands with a fork, set aside. You can use the shell for serving if desired..
  2. In a medium nonstick pan, brown the turkey for 5 minutes. Drain and return to the pan. Add the cumin, chili, garlic, and oregano; stir well, cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 10 minutes..
  3. Sprinkle the spaghetti strands with S&P and place in a 9x13 casserole dish or the emptied shells..
  4. Pour the turkey chili mixture over the squash. Sprinkle with 1/2C shredded cheese. Spoon the yogurt over the chili and sprinkle with the remaining cheese. Bake for 30 minutes or until heated through..
