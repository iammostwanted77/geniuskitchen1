Ingredients of Louisiana Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

  1. Prepare 1/3 cup of flour.
  2. You need 1/3 cup of vegetable oil.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of chopped onion (1 medium).
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of chopped green or red sweet pepper (1 small).
  5. You need 1/2 cup of sliced celery (1 stalk).
  6. Prepare 3 clove of garlic, minced.
  7. You need 1 1/2-2 tsp of Cajun seasoning.
  8. It's 1 can (14.5 oz) of beef broth.
  9. It's 3/4 cup of water.
  10. It's 10 oz of frozen cut okra or cut green beans.
  11. You need 1 1/2 cup of chopped cooked chicken.
  12. Prepare 8 oz of cooked smoked sausage links, sliced.
  13. You need 3 cup of hot cooked long grain white or brown rice.
  14. You need 1 of sliced green onions (optional).
  15. It's 1 bottle of hot pepper sauce (optional).

Louisiana Chicken and Sausage Gumbo step by step

  1. For Roux, in a 3-quart heavy saucepan combine flour and oil until smooth. Cook over medium high heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to medium. Cook, stirring constantly, for 8 to 10 minutes or until Roux is dark reddish brown..
  2. Stir in 1/2 cup onion, sweet pepper, celery, and garlic. Cook about 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring frequently. Stir in Cajun seasoning. Add broth and the water; stir in okra. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, covered, for 15 minutes. Stir in chicken and sausage; heat through..
  3. Serve gumbo in bowls with hot cooked rice. If desired, top with green onions and serve with hot pepper sauce..
