Ingredients of Cucumber macaroni salad

  1. It's 16 oz of elbow macaroni.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  3. It's 1 cup of Cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of dried minced onion.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes.
  6. It's 1 cup of diced ham.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of peas.
  9. It's 3 tbsp of olive oil.
  10. You need 3 tbsp of white wine vinegar.
  11. Prepare 1 dash of salt and pepper.
  12. It's 1 tsp of dill weed.

Cucumber macaroni salad instructions

  1. Cook elbow macaroni according to package directions..
  2. Rinse under cold water and drain..
  3. In a large bowl, combine the elbow macaroni and mayonnaise; mix well..
  4. Stir in cucumber, onions, tomatoes, ham, cheese and peas..
  5. Mix well..
  6. Drizzle oil and vinegar over the top, season with salt and pepper, and toss..
  7. Sprinkle dill weed over the top..
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours..
