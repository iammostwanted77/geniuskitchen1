Ingredients of Puerto Ricanized macaroni salad

Puerto Ricanized macaroni salad step by step

  1. in a large bowl marinate your chicken with vinegar, adobo, garlic powder, sazon and set aside.
  2. in a pan heat oil on medium heat, add Sofrito, and pimiento and saute until oil starts to turn green.
  3. add chicken and saute until chicken is fully cooked..
  4. boil macaroni as directed on box in very salty water. Once finished rinse in very cold water..
  5. chop green bell peppers and onions and add to macaroni.
  6. add chicken with drippings and mayo. Mix together and serve at room temperature.
