Shrimp Cajun Gumbo step by step

  1. Cooked white rice in large heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt 1 cup butter.
  2. Add the flour, and cook on low heat until the roux is dark brown (30-45 minutes stirring constantly).
  3. Add onion, celery, peppers and garlic. Saut√© until it's translucent.
  4. Mix in okra and remaining ingredients including 1 tbls of additional butter..
  5. Simmer over med-low heat until thick, about 1 hour.
  6. Remove bay leaves, and serve.
