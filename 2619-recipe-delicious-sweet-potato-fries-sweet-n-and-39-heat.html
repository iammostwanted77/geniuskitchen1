Sweet Potato Fries-Sweet n' Heat step by step

  1. Carefully Slice Potatos into strips, wedges or circles..
  2. Place on a cooking sheet and coat and toss with all ingredients.
  3. Warm oven to 375 and then bake for 15 mins on. tossing fries every 5 minutes.
  4. Check fries for tenderness. Break one open and test. Done when center is soft. May need to cook for additional 5-9 mins..
  5. Salt to taste. Enjoy.
