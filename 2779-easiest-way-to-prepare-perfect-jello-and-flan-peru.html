Jello and flan (Peru) instructions

  1. Make jello and flan as per instructions on box.
  2. Pour flan halfway into several cups and refrigerate. Pour jello into bowl and cool in fridge..
  3. 1 hour later pour cooled jello into halfway filled flan cups until full..
  4. Leave overnight or wait until jello is ready (3-4hr).
