Sweet Potato Cake that Tastes Like Japanese Sweet Potato Cake (Twice-Baked Sweet Potato Confections)! step by step

  1. Line the mold with parchment paper..
  2. Boil the skinned sweet potatoes, mash while hot, then add the ● sugar and mix..
  3. When the mixture cools down, add the ● egg yolk and the ● milk and mix..
  4. Combine the ※ egg whites and ※ sugar in a separate bowl and whisk..
  5. Add 1/3 of the egg whites from Step 4 to the batter from Step 3, then thoroughly mix. Add the remainder of the egg whites, then fold them into the batter in a cutting motion..
  6. Add the melted butter to the batter..
  7. Pour the batter into a lined mold, and tap the base several times to release any trapped air. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in an oven preheated to 170°C..
