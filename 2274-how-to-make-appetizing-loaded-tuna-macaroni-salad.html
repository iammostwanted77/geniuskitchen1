Ingredients of Loaded Tuna Macaroni Salad

  1. Prepare 1 of -16 oz. box of pasta, spiral or bowtie.
  2. You need 1 medium of purple onion.
  3. Prepare 1/2 each of of red, yellow, & orange bell peppers.
  4. You need 2 can of of solid white tuna drained.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of Hellmans mayo (or more or less to your liking).
  6. It's 2 tsp of sweet pickle relish.
  7. It's 1 small of splash of vinegar.
  8. It's 1 of tiny squirt of yellow mustard.
  9. Prepare 3 of boiled eggs.
  10. It's 1 of original Mrs. Dash.
  11. It's 1 of garlic salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  12. It's 1 of sprinkle of parsley flakes.

Loaded Tuna Macaroni Salad instructions

  1. Boil the pasta, drain water and shock pasta with cold water to stop cooking. Place cooked pasta in a separate mixing bowl..
  2. Chop the bell peppers and onion, add to the pasta. Add mayo, vinegar, mustard, Mrs Dash, garlic salt and pepper and parsley flakes to the pasta as well..
  3. Open and drain the 2 cans of tuna. Flake the tuna with a fork, then add to pasta. Peel and slice the boiled eggs, add to pasta..
  4. Now that everything is put together in the mixing bowl, toss well until everything is coated nicely and salad looks creamy..
  5. Serve and eat right away, or chill in refrigerator until ready to eat..
