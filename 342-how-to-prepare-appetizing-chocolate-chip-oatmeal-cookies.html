  1. Prepare 100 g of Canola oil or Rapeseed oil.
  2. It's 200 g of Honey.
  3. Prepare 100-150 g of Chocolate Chips (or / and raisins).
  4. You need 200 g of All purpose flour.
  5. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  6. You need 2.5 tsp of Baking soda.
  7. Prepare 300 g of Oatmeal.
  8. Prepare 120-130 cc of Water.
