Ingredients of Crock Pot Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

  1. Prepare 1/3 cup of all-purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup of oil.
  3. You need 3 cup of water.
  4. You need 12 oz of cooked sausage links, sliced.
  5. It's 1/2 cup of diced cooked chicken (from my weekly roast chicken).
  6. Prepare 2 cup of sliced okra or 10-ounce package frozen okra.
  7. You need 1 cup of onion, diced (about 1 large).
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of green pepper, diced.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of celery, diced.
  10. It's 4 clove of garlic, minced.
  11. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  12. You need 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper.
  13. You need 1/4 tsp of ground red pepper.
  14. It's 3 cup of hot cooked rice.

Crock Pot Chicken and Sausage Gumbo instructions

  1. To make the roux, stir together oil and flour in a medium saucepan until smooth..
  2. Cook over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly..
  3. Reduce heat to medium; cook and stir constantly about 15 minutes more until it’s a dark, reddish-brown color. Cool..
  4. Place water in a Crock Pot and add roux; stir..
  5. Add sausage, chicken, okra, onion, pepper, celery, garlic, salt, black pepper, and red pepper..
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-7 hours, or on high heat for 3 hours..
  7. Skim off the fat, and serve over rice..
