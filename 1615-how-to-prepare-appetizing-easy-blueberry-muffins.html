Easy Blueberry Muffins.

You can cook Easy Blueberry Muffins using 9 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Easy Blueberry Muffins

  1. It's 1 1/2 cups of flour.
  2. It's 3/4 cup of sugar.
  3. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of baking powder.
  5. It's 1/3 cup of vegetable oil.
  6. It's 1 of large egg.
  7. It's 1/3-1/2 cup of milk.
  8. You need 1 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  9. Prepare 6-8 oz of blueberries, fresh or frozen.

Easy Blueberry Muffins step by step

  1. Heat oven to 400 deg F. Line muffin tin with paper liners or oil/butter.
  2. Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Add oil to a measuring cup that at least holds 1 cup. Add the egg then fill the cup with milk to the 1 cup line. Add vanilla and whisk to combine.
  4. Add milk mixture to dry ingredients then use a fork to combine. Do not overmix.
  5. Put batter in muffin tins. For cups with no batter put 1-2 tbsp of water for even cooking.
  6. Bake for 15-20 min or until a toothpick inserted into it will come out clean..
  7. Store in a plastic bag at room temperature for 2-3 days..
  8. To freeze, wrap them in aluminum foil or place in freezer bags. Freeze for up to 3 months.
