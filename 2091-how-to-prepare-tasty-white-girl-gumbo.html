Ingredients of White Girl Gumbo

  1. Prepare 1 cup of olive oil.
  2. It's 1 cup of flour.
  3. It's 1 of green bell pepper, diced.
  4. Prepare 1 of red bell pepper, diced.
  5. It's 3 of jalapeño, diced.
  6. You need 1 medium of onion, chopped.
  7. You need 3 of stalks celery, diced.
  8. You need 1 packages of smoked sausage (Louisiana if you can find it).
  9. It's 3 tsp of minced garlic.
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp of creole or Cajun seasoning.
  11. Prepare 6 cup of chicken broth, add more if you need to thin it out more as you go along.
  12. Prepare 1 of rotisserie chicken (3-4 lb).
  13. Prepare 4 cup of cooked rice, I prefer jasmine.

White Girl Gumbo step by step

  1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven or large pot. When oil is hot whisk flour in slowly..
  2. Continue whisking until a roux is formed and becomes the color of light chocolate milk. This takes approx 8 mins. Any black flecks in it and you need to start over..
  3. The roux is fairly thin right now. Don't worry, the next step thickens it right quick..
  4. Stir onion, peppers, celery and sausage into the roux. Cook five minutes..
  5. Add garlic, stir and cook another 5 minutes..
  6. Add creole/Cajun seasoning mix thoroughly..
  7. Pour in the chicken broth and give it a stir. Bring to a slow boil and reduce heat. Simmer uncovered 15 minutes. Stir often to avoid sticking to bottom of pan..
  8. Add your ripped apart chicken, stir and cook another 15 minutes. Stir and now is when you can decide if you need a bit more garlic or to add salt. I find smoked salt is best..
  9. Ladle on top of your cooked rice..
