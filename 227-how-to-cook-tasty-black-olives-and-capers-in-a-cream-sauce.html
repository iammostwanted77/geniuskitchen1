Ingredients of Black Olives and Capers in a Cream Sauce

  1. Prepare 4 tablespoons of butter.
  2. You need 4 cloves of garlic finelly chopped.
  3. You need 1 of bay leaf.
  4. Prepare 3 cups of heavy whipping cream.
  5. It's 1/2 teaspoon of white pepper.
  6. You need 4 tablespoons of cold milk (optional).
  7. It's 1 tablespoon of corn starch (optional).
  8. You need 1 cup of black olives pitted finely chopped.
  9. You need 1 tablespoon of finely chopped capers.
  10. It's of Salt (to taste).
  11. Prepare of Parmesan cheese (to taste).
