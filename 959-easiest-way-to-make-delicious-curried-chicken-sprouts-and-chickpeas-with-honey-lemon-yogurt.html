Curried chicken, sprouts and chickpeas with honey lemon yogurt instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180c.
  2. Cut the sprouts in half and the shallots into slices. Place in a roasting tin.
  3. Drain and rinse the chickpeas. Place in the tray along with the chicken..
  4. Toss the spice mix together along with the ingredients in the tray with some olive oil..
  5. Roast for 20mins till golden. Squeeze over the lemon juice, sprinkle with pistachios and return to the oven for 5mins.
  6. Mix together the yogurt, honey and lemon juice to make a dressing..
  7. Serve the chicken topped with the yogurt dressing. ðŸ˜‹.
