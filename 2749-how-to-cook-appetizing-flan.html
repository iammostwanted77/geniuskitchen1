Flan step by step

  1. Set oven at 325°.
  2. Blend the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, milk, eggs and vanilla extract. Blend cream cheese and corn starch after. Fat free ingredients will have different results.
  3. Put sugar in a pan, stir constantly and let melt. Do not let boil.
  4. Pour melted sugar in baking dish or single baking cups.
  5. Once sugar has cooled, pour blended ingredients into dish.
  6. Place dish in water bath.
  7. Place in oven for hour and a half or until top is golden brown.
  8. Let cool in fridge.
  9. Run thin plastic knife around flan to release from dish. Flip onto plate or platter.
