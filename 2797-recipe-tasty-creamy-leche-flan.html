Creamy Leche Flan instructions

  1. Separate egg yolk and egg white.
  2. Combine egg yolk, fresh milk & condensed milk, mix it using a hand mixer..
  3. In a leche flan pan put sugar and caramelize it..
  4. Using a cotton cloth pour on the mixture to separate bubbles and excess lumps..
  5. Pour on the mixture in the pan and steam it for about 20 mins..
