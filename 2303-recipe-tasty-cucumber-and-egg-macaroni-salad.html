Ingredients of Cucumber and Egg Macaroni Salad

  1. Prepare 1 (16 oz) of box Macaroni (I used Muellers).
  2. It's 7 of hard boiled eggs.
  3. You need 1 of large yellow onion.
  4. Prepare 1 of cucumber (keep skin on you prefer).
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of sweet relish.
  6. You need 1 cup of plus two tbs of mayonnaise (I used Duke Mayonnaise).
  7. It's 1/2 tbs of mustard.
  8. You need 1 1/2 tsp of apple cider vinegar.
  9. You need 2 pinches of celery salt.
  10. Prepare 2-3 pinches of onion powder.
  11. You need of small pinch of paprika.
  12. Prepare of small pinch of red pepper.
  13. Prepare of salt for taste.
  14. Prepare of pepper for taste.

Cucumber and Egg Macaroni Salad step by step

  1. Cook the macaroni until package directions. Drain the noodles and set aside. While macaroni is cooking, rinse the cucumber and slice or remove the skin then slice then half each slice. Dice up the hard boiled eggs..
  2. Pour the Macaroni into a large bowl. Add the rest of the ingredients along with the cucumbers and eggs. Mix Well. Add more seasoning if needed. Cover and place in the fridge for 2-4 hours before serving..
