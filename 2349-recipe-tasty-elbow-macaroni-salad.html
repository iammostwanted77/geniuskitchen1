Ingredients of Elbow Macaroni Salad

  1. Prepare 4 Cups of uncooked elbow macaroni.
  2. It's 1 Cup of ham, diced.
  3. You need 1 Cup of celery stalks, sliced.
  4. It's 1/2 Cup of green onion, chopped.
  5. It's 1/4 Cup of sweet pickle relish (substitute lemon juice or ACV).
  6. Prepare 1 Jar (2 oz) of diced pimientos, drained.
  7. You need 4 of eggs, hard-boiled, sliced.
  8. Prepare of Dressing mixture.
  9. Prepare 1 Cup of salad dressing or mayonnaise.
  10. It's 2 TBS of yellow mustard.
  11. You need 1/2 TSP of salt.
  12. Prepare 1/4 TSP of black pepper, ground.

Elbow Macaroni Salad step by step

  1. Cook and drain macaroni as directed on package. Rinse with cold water, drain well and refrigerate for 1 hour..
  2. In large mixing bowl, add macaroni, ham, celery, onion, pickle relish, and pimientos.
  3. In a different bowl mix mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper..
  4. Add noodles to sauce/dressing. Gently stir in sliced boiled eggs. Cover and refrigerate overnight..
