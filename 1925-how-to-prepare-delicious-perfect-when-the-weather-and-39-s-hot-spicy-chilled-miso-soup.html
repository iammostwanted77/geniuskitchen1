Ingredients of Perfect When the Weather's Hot! Spicy Chilled Miso Soup

  1. Prepare 1/2 of Cucumber (thinly sliced).
  2. Prepare 2 of Cherry tomatoes (quartered).
  3. It's 40 grams of Canned tuna.
  4. It's 200 ml of Plain cooked rice.
  5. It's 1 dash of Toasted white sesame seeds and salt.
  6. It's 1 tsp of Sesame oil.
  7. Prepare of For the soup:.
  8. It's 200 ml of Bonito dashi stock.
  9. You need 1 tsp of Miso.
  10. Prepare 1/3 tsp of Doubanjiang.

Perfect When the Weather's Hot! Spicy Chilled Miso Soup instructions

  1. Heat the dashi stock in a pan. Dissolve the miso and doubanjiang. Transfer to a container and chill in the refrigerator..
  2. Sprinkle cucumber slices with a little salt and leave for 10 minutes. Squeeze out tightly. Combine with the tuna..
  3. Put rice in a bowl and pour the chilled soup over it. Add the cherry tomatoes plus the cucumber-tuna mix from Step 2, and sprinkle sesame seeds. Drizzle sesame oil to finish..
