Orzo macaroni salad instructions

  1. add 1 Tbspoon to orzo stir to make fluffy..
  2. put juices from fruits in pot and put the fruits in seperate containers in refridgerator.
  3. put juice on stove and add sugar,flour and salt.
  4. cook till it boils then add well beaten eggs,and stir until thick.
  5. let cool then pour over the orzo macaroni.
  6. let this sit in refrigerator till morning.
  7. in morning add a large container of cool whip and add your pineapple and oranges.
