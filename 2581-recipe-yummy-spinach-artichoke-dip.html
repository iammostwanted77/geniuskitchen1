Ingredients of Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. You need 1 of Kosher Salt.
  2. It's 2 each of 10-ounce bags spinach, stems removed.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of Unsalted butter.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of Minced Onion.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of All purpose flour.
  6. It's 1 1/4 cup of Whole Milk.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Worcestershire sauce.
  8. It's 1/4 cup of Sour Cream.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of frozen artichoke hearts, thawed, squeezed dry, and roughly chopped.
  10. Prepare 1 of tortilla chips.
  11. You need 1 clove of Garlic - minced.
