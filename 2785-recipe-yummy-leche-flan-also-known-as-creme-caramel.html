Ingredients of Leche Flan (also known as crème caramel)

  1. Prepare 1 can of evaporated milk.
  2. Prepare 1 can of condense milk.
  3. Prepare 5 tsp of of white or brown sugar.
  4. You need 5 tsp of water.
  5. It's 6 large of eggs.

This delicious dessert is known throughout the world. It has been a regular item in the menu of most restaurants because of its taste, ease in preparation and long shelf life. The world calls this dessert Spanish flan but, in the Philippines, it is leche flan. Leche, Spanish for milk, is one of the dessert's two main ingredients.

Leche Flan (also known as crème caramel) instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375°F Fahrenheit.
  2. mix evaporated milk, condense milk and eggs in a bowl until it's smooth.
  3. mix sugar and water in a cooking pot..
  4. put the cooking pot on the stove..
  5. cook sugar and water. mix it making sure the sugar melts evenly..
  6. pour the sugar and water mixture into the leche flan pan..
  7. spread the sugar around the leche flan pan evenly.
  8. pour the milk and egg mixture into the leche flan pan.
  9. put the leche flan pan into a cake pan that is 1/2 full of water..
  10. put it all into the oven..
  11. bake at 375°F Fahrenheit for an hour.
  12. once it is done baking let it cool down..
  13. flip the leche flan pan upside down on to your serving plate to get the leche flan out. serve and enjoy!.
