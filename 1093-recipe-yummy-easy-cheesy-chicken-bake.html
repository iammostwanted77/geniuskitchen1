Ingredients of Easy cheesy chicken bake

  1. You need 3 cup of uncooked macaroni.
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of diced chicken breast raw or precooked (Suggested and preferred for simplicity).
  3. Prepare 1 lb of cheddar cheese.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of milk.
  5. It's 1/2 tbsp of salt.
  6. It's 1 tsp of black pepper.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of butter or margarine.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of Additional various cheeses of your choice (optional).

Easy cheesy chicken bake instructions

  1. Boil macaroni as directed on box then strain and set aside.
  2. Thaw precooked chicken or if using raw, proceed to cook chicken, season to taste, then dice it in small chunks. Set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  4. On low heat, add milk, butter, salt, pepper and one pound of cheddar slowly into pot. Stir frequently to prevent scorching and sticking..
  5. Macaroni can be seasoned to taste at this time..
  6. Add chicken to creamy cheese mixture. Continue to stir..
  7. Optional: This would be a good time to add veggies such as chopped broccoli or chopped asparagus..
  8. Pour mixture in casserole dish. Top with remaining cheeses of your choice. I used a combination of pizza blend and mild cheddar.
  9. Bake in oven 15-20 minutes without browning. ENJOY!.
