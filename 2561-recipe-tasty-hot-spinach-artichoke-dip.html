Ingredients of Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. Prepare 1 Package of Frozen Chopped Spinach.
  2. Prepare 1 Can of Artichoke Hearts.
  3. You need 8 oz of Cream Cheese.
  4. You need 1/4 Cup of Sour Cream.
  5. You need 1/4 Cup of Mayonnaise.
  6. You need 2/3 Cup of Parmesan.
  7. You need 1/2 Cup of Mozzarella Cheese.
  8. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon of Red Pepper Fakes.
  9. It's 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt.
  10. It's 1/4 Teaspoon of Garlic Powder.

Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. Place cream cheese in microwave for 1 minute to thaw..
  3. Place frozen chopped spinach in microwave in a bowl with 2 tablespoons of water and cook for 4 minutes..
  4. Remove spinach from the microwave and strain out as much water as posibble by squeezing in your hands, careful its not too hot..
  5. Blend all ingredients in a bowl together with the exception of 1/3 Parmesan cheese..
  6. Spray an oven safe 8x8 oven safe pan, or a pan close to that size with cooking spray..
  7. Add mixture to pan and sprinkle remaining 1/3 cup parmesan cheese on top..
  8. Place in oven and cook for 30 minutes. The chese should be a golden color on top..
