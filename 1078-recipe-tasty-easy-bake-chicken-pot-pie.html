Easy bake chicken pot pie. instructions

  1. Boil chicken breast in broth with vegetable and chicken stock until done, shred chicken and let cool saving chicken broth..
  2. Chop carrot, celery, and onion ad to chicken mix in broth..
  3. As salt, pepper, garlic bring to a boil, then let set..
  4. Grease a 9inch pie pan with butter and spread half the chicken mix in pan..
  5. Cut pie crust into strips and layer onto chicken mix. Ad another layer of chicken, then whole pie crust over chicken mix..
  6. Bake at 425 for 20-25 minutes, let set for 10 then enjoy..
