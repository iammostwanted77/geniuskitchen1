Blueberry oatmeal cookies step by step

  1. Rinse blueberries then heat in sausepan on medium heat with 2c water, ginger, and sugar for 20min. Stir often..
  2. Add honey and stir well until loose sause, having bits of berry is fine but liquid should be thicker than water..
  3. Remove from heat, encorporate vanilla extraxt then let cool..
  4. Stir well with oats and let sit for 8hrs or overnight in air tight container..
  5. When ready to cook, heat oven to 325°. Add nuts and chips to oats, stir well..
  6. Beat egg then add to oat berry mixture..
  7. Use two spoons to make cookies on cookie sheet..
  8. Bake for 20 minutes, check cookies and cook longer as preferred..
