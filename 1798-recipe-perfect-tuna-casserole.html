  9. You need 2 slice of pepperjack cheese.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of shredded cheese.
  11. It's 1 small of bag of potato chips for topping.

Tuna Casserole instructions

  1. Boil noodles until tender (8 to 12 min).
  2. While noodles are boiling, mix together in microwaveable bowl : drained tuna, soup, milk, seasoning, torn up pepperjack cheese, & seasonings. cover, then microwave for roughly 2min or until warm throughout..
  3. Drain noodles, place in 13x9 pan. Pour tuna mixture in & mix together. Sprinkle with shredded cheese & green onions..
  4. Crunch up potato chips, and sprinkle a layer on top of whole casserole. Bake on 350 for about 8 to 10min..
  5. I serve with homeade garlic bread!.
