Chicken and sausage gumbo step by step

  1. Add flour and oil, whisk and stir constantly until it turns color of chocolate (20-25 minutes).
  2. Mixed in diced vegetables for 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken, sausage, garlic and seasonings.
  4. Add chicken broth, corn, okra, and tomatoes.
  5. Bring to boil and let simmer on low/medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve over rice.
