Ingredients of twice baked potato

  1. Prepare 1 of potatoes.
  2. You need 1 of butter.
  3. You need 1 of sour cream.
  4. You need 1 of green onions.
  5. Prepare 1 of bacon bits.
  6. Prepare 1 packages of shredded cheese.

twice baked potato instructions

  1. heat oven to 400.
  2. bake your potatoes til they are soft.
  3. take them out, cut them open, and take the insides out and put in seperate bowl.
  4. set outsides of potatoes aside.
  5. add butter and sour cream to your liking of taste in bowl.
  6. chop up a few green onions (optional) and add them also to the bowl.
  7. now add your bacon bits.
  8. mix well.
  9. take ur mixture and put it back into the skins of ur potatoes. be sure to try to use it all when u put it back in skins. if not that's ok..
  10. take shredded cheese and completely cover tops of potatoes.
  11. put back in oven til cheese on top melts.
  12. then take them out of oven and enjoy.
