Rob's Spicy Mexican Soup Gumbo instructions

  1. Make Instant White Rice per instructions on box.
  2. Brown ground beef/turkey in skillet.
  3. Cook Tortilla soup per instructions on package.
  4. Serve as follows per serving: Put white rice & ground beef in bowl..
  5. Pour at least 1 laddle of Tortilla soup over rice & beef combo..
  6. Put couple large tablespoons of salsa/pico de gallo in the bowl..
  7. Put a dolup of sour cream on top of salsa.
  8. Cover with shredded cheese & tortilla chips. Then serve..
  9. (Optional) Add choice of hot sauce, I prefer Cholula..
