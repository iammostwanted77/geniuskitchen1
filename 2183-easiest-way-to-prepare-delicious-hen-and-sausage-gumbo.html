Hen and sausage gumbo step by step

  1. fry hen to a deep brown in hot oil then remove from oil.
  2. add hen to 3 quarts boiling water and add vegetables boil until meat is tender about 1 1/2 hours.
  3. boil sausage in 1 quart of water for 20 minutes.
  4. add sausage and water to hen and vegetables boil 30 minutes more.
  5. remove from heat. add file'.
  6. serve over hot rice.
