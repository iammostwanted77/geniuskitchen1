Ingredients of Blueberries Muffins

  1. You need 1 3/4 cup of White flour.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Sugar.
  3. You need 3 tsp of Baking powder.
  4. It's 1/3 tsp of Salt.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of Milk.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of Plain yogurt.
  7. You need 1 of Egg.
  8. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla extract.
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup of Vegetable oil.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of Blueberries.

Blueberries Muffins instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Line 12cup baking sheet with paper cups.
  3. In one bowl mix dry ingredients.
  4. In other bowl mix milk, yogurt and the egg. Beat to blend well with a whisker.
  5. Add the dry ingredients in once. Mix with spoon gently. Add dusted blueberries, not over mix..
  6. Fill cups to the top bake for 18 minutes.
