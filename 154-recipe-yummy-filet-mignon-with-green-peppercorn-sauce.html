Ingredients of Filet mignon with green peppercorn sauce

  1. You need 15 grams of unsalted butter.
  2. You need 20 ml of brandy.
  3. You need 1 tbsp of mustard.
  4. It's 2 of beef tenderloins (200g each ca.).
  5. You need 1 tbsp of green peppercorn in brine.
  6. Prepare 200 ml of double cream/heavy cream.

Filet mignon with green peppercorn sauce instructions

  1. (Optional) Use butcher's twine to tie up the tenderloins in a round shape, this will help them cook evenly.
  2. Crush 3/4 of the peppercorns with a meat hammer or a rolling pin.
  3. Press peppercorns on the tenderloins, both sides. Save some of them for later..
  4. Melt butter on a frying pan and cook tenderloins as you like it. I like them medium, 4 minutes each side.
  5. Keep the tenderloins warm (on a hot plate or under an aluminum foil).
  6. Pour brandy and mustard in the same frying pan you used before.
  7. Stir and cook on low heat for 1 minute.
  8. Add double cream and the green peppercorns you saved before.
  9. Cook on low heat until thicken.
  10. Put the tenderloins in the frying pan with the sauce you just made, few seconds are enough.
