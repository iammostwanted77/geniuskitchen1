Blueberry Muffins.

You can have Blueberry Muffins using 9 ingredients and 13 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Blueberry Muffins

  1. It's 2 cup of Blueberries.
  2. It's 2 cup of All Purpose Flour.
  3. It's 3 tsp of Baking Powder.
  4. You need 6 tbsp of Butter or Margerine.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of Brown Sugar.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of White sugar.
  7. It's 1 of Egg.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  9. You need 7 oz of Milk.

Blueberry Muffins instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F.
  2. Prepare cup cake pan . Spraying cooking spray is my preference or place cups ..
  3. Measure 2 cups of fresh blueberries or a 12 oz bag of frozen blueberries .Set aside 36 for garnish . If you are using frozen , cover remaining berries with tap water to defrost . Set aside for later ..
  4. Sift or mix well the Flour and Baking Power , then set aside ..
  5. Cream together the butter or margarine , brown sugar and white sugar ..
  6. Add egg and vanilla and cream in the mix ..
  7. Stir in milk , scraping the sides of the bowl to get well mixed ..
  8. Mix in dry ingredients , just enough till you don't see any flour ..
  9. Drain the water from the blueberries and fold them into the batter . Scrape from the bottom carefully lifting up not to smash the berries ..
  10. Fill the cups with even amounts . Put a spoon of batter in each cup , then go back to each to assure each muffins will have an equal amount of berries ..
  11. Garnish muffins tops with remaining blueberries and push down even with batter level ..
  12. Sprinkle tops with sugar , light to heavy depending on your preference . The sugar adds a slight crunch to the tops ..
  13. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes at 375°F , till tops are browned . It had enough liquid in it so it can have a crunch on top and moist inside ..
