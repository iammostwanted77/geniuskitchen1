Ingredients of Mixed wok in 15min

  1. You need 300 gm of small chicken sausages.
  2. Prepare 200 gm of veggie meatballs (pre made).

  4. It's 2 cloves of garlic (minced).
  5. You need 1 teaspoon of cooking oil.
  6. Prepare 1 pinch of chili flakes.
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon of soya sauce.
  8. It's 0.5 dl of water.
  9. It's to taste of Salt and pepper.

Mixed wok in 15min step by step

  1. Heat up frying pan and pour in the cooking oil..
  2. Add in the garlic minced. Stir until almost golden color. Then add the sausage and pre made meatballs and wok it in high heat. After 5 min quickly add in the mini Cabbage..
  3. Continue wok in high heat. Then add in the water continue stir until smell little bit burn (that's called wok up) then add the soya sauce, chili flakes and salt & pepper..
  4. Whole process only take 15min. Quick and tasty..
  5. Tips: served with apple salad would be the best choice😋.
