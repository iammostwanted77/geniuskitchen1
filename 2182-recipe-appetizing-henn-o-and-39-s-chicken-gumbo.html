Henn O's Chicken Gumbo instructions

  1. Heat 4quart pot w/ olive oil.
  2. Add 3tsp flour.
  3. Add pinch of salt and black pepper.
  4. Stir mixture until flour has become smooth..
  5. 1 Tbsp of butter.
  6. 14oz of Chicken broth or stock.
  7. 2 Tbsp of File.
  8. Stir mixture until creamy and desired taste is achieved..
  9. Finely chop 1 lrg valdelia onion, 1 red bell pepper, 1 green bell pepper, 2 cellary sticks.
  10. Cut 3 ears of corn into small quarters.
  11. Cut 4-5 oakra into small section.
  12. Add all ingredients to your rue, stir continously until mixture has started to thicken..
  13. Reduce flame to allow pot to simmer.
  14. Cut 2 chicken breast into small 1/4 bites and pan fry in olive oil until a golden brown crust is achieved..
  15. Cut 6 chicken sausage into bite size bits and pan fry in olive oil.
  16. Combine your chicken mixture to your stock and continue stirring pot slowly to prevent burning..
  17. Add 1/2 cup of Hennessey liqour to mixture and cover w/ lid.
  18. Allow mixture to simmer for 15 mins, allowing all ingredients to marinate with each other..
