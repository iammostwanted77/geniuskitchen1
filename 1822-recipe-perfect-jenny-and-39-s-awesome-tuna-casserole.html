Jenny's Awesome Tuna Casserole step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Boil Egg Noodles. Follow package directions. Drain..
  3. Peel, wash, and chop Onion..
  4. In a large sauce pan or skillet, heat soup, milk, mayo, onions, peas, tuna, black pepper, and a 1\2 cup of the cheddar cheese on med/low heat..
  5. Heat until cheese is melted and mixed thoroughly, about 5-10 mins. Fold in egg noodles..
  6. In a large casserole dish, spread mixture evenly..
  7. Spread the remaining 1 1/2 cups of cheese, evenly on top of mixture..
  8. On the same burner, heat butter in a skillet on med/low. Once butter has melted, pour Panko crumbs in pan..
  9. Season the bread crumbs, and stir frequently. (About 3-5 mins.).
  10. Remove from heat, and pour evenly over casserole..
  11. Bake for 40-45 mins, or until top is a nice golden brown..
