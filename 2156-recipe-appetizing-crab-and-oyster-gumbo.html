Ingredients of Crab and oyster gumbo

  1. It's 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of vegetable oil.
  3. Prepare 1 lb of Andouille Sausage, sliced 1/4 inch thick.
  4. You need 3 of Celery Ribs, cut into 1/2 inch dice.
  5. Prepare 1 of Onion, cut into 1/2 inch dice.
  6. You need 1 of Red bell pepper, cut into 1/2 inch dice.
  7. You need 1 of Habanero chile, minced and most seeds discarded.
  8. Prepare 3 of Garlic cloves, minced.
  9. You need 1/2 lb of okra, sliced 1/4 inch thick.
  10. Prepare 2 tsp of Dried thyme.
  11. It's 1 of Bay leaf.
  12. It's 3 tbsp of Gumbo file.
  13. You need 5 cup of Chicken stock.
  14. Prepare 3 cup of Bottled clam juice.
  15. Prepare 3 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce.
  16. It's 3 large of Tomatoes, fully chopped.
  17. Prepare 1 lb of lump crabmeat, picked over.
  18. It's 24 of Shucked oysters and their liquor.
  19. You need 1 of Salt and freshly ground peppet.

Crab and oyster gumbo instructions

  1. In a pot, stir the flour and oil until smooth. Cook over moderate heat, stirring often, until the roux turns a rich brown color, 15 minutes. Add the andouille, celery, onion, bell pepper, habanero, garlic, okra, thyme, bay leaf, and half of the gumbo file, and cook over moderate heat, stirring until the onion is translucent. Add the stock, clam juice, worcestershire and tomatoes; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 hour, stirring..
  2. Stir in the remaining gumbo file and add the crab, oysters and their liquor. Season with salt and pepper and simmer gently for 1 minute to just cook the oysters..
