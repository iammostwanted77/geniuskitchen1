thomas flan instructions

  2. add melted butter..
  3. mix the two ingredients..
  4. add to flan case or a round tin with a maximum height of 2-3 cm..

  7. add 1 carton of double cream to the milk..
  8. add in some lemon zest..
  9. add lemon juice..
  10. whisk all ingredients together until the mixture is evenly mixed..
  11. pour the mixture on th top of the biscuit..
  12. level the mixture with a spoon..
