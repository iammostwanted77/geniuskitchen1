Mushroom & capers Omelet instructions

  1. Peel and slice the onion (thin)..
  2. Fry the sliced onion in a little hot oil..
  3. When the onion starts turning brown add the diced mushrooms and cook for 5 min..
  4. Scramble the eggs, the capers and the seasoning. Add the mixture to the frying pan..
  5. Cook over medium heat until the eggs are no longer runny..
