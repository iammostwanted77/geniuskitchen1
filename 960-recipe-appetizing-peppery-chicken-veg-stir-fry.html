Ingredients of Peppery Chicken Veg Stir Fry

  1. You need 4 Pieces of Chicken Fillets cubed (boneless and skinless).
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 of Tblsp Ginger and Garlic Paste.
  3. It's 125 g of Butter.
  4. You need to taste of Salt.
  5. It's 2 tsp of Fresh Ground Black Pepper.
  6. Prepare 1 of Medium Chopped Onion.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 Cup of Fresh Cauliflower.
  8. It's 1 Cup of Fresh Broccoli.
  9. You need 1 Cup of Fresh Shredded Kale.
  10. You need 1/2 Cup of Brussels Sprouts.
  11. Prepare 1 Cup of Fresh Sweet Corn.

Peppery Chicken Veg Stir Fry step by step

  1. To your pan cook your onions until golden brown..
  2. After, add in your chicken along with your ginger and garlic paste. Cook till slightly browned..
  3. Serve with brown rice and top with fresh parsley..
  4. Add in all your vegetables at once. Season with salt and pepper..
