Stuffed mushroom cups step by step

  1. Clean mushrooms, clean from a black part of the cup.
  2. Cut the "leg" of the mushrooms.
  3. Chop it.
  4. Also chop hot dogs and paperoni.
  5. Mix with mushrooms.
  6. Put mushroom cups in the pan, feel with a little bit mayo and ketchup.
  7. Mix them.
  8. Fill cups with your mix.
  9. Put mozzarella on top and bake 20 min on 400°F.
  10. Enjoy.
