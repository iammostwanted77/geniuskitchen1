Ingredients of Fresh Blueberry Muffins

  1. It's 1/2 cup of Butter (room Temp.).
  2. It's 1 cup of Sugar.
  3. It's 2 large of Eggs.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of Baking Powder.
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Salt.
  7. It's 2 1/2 cup of Blueberries (mash 1/2 W/ Fork).
  8. You need 2 cup of Flour.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of Milk.
  10. You need 1 tbsp of Sugar Mixed W. 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg.
